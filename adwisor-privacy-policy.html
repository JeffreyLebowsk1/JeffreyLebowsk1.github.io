Adwisor — CCCC Advisor Lookup Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Adwisor is an advisor lookup Chrome extension for Central Carolina Community College (CCCC), developed by zappad.MD. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Adwisor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. The extension runs entirely within your browser.

Permissions

The extension accesses only publicly available CCCC advisor directory information to display lookup results. No personal user data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Adwisor does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Storage

No user data is stored locally or remotely by the extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at zappad.md@gmail.com.
