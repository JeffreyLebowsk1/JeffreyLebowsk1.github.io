Qoder — QR Code Generator Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Qoder is a QR code generator Chrome extension developed by zappad.MD. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Qoder does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. The extension runs entirely within your browser.

Permissions

The extension uses the activeTab permission solely to read the current page URL and pre-fill it into the QR code input field. No page content, browsing history, or other data is accessed or stored.

Third-Party Services

Qoder does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser.

Data Storage

No user data is stored locally or remotely by the extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at zappad.md@gmail.com.
